Looking to upgrade your drawers with soft closing features? A soft-close drawer slide is a must-have in every modern home. It not only provides a quiet and smooth operation but also prevents your drawers from slamming shut. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to install soft close drawer slides.

Pain Points of Installing Soft Close Drawer Slides

One of the biggest issues people face while installing soft close drawer slides is not having the appropriate tools or hardware. It’s crucial to have the right items for a smooth installation process. Additionally, it can be hard to understand the installation instructions for a beginner. Even if you have installed regular drawers before, installing soft close drawer slides can be a bit more complicated.

How to Install Soft Close Drawer Slides

Before starting, you need to determine the type of drawer slide you need. Soft close drawer slides come in full extension, partial extension, and undermount options. Once you have decided which type you need, gather all the necessary hardware and tools.

The first step is to remove the old drawer slides. Next, attach the cabinet members and drawer members according to the instructions. Ensure that the slides are level and centered. Finally, attach the drawer front and test the drawer slide.

Summary of Main Points

To summarize, the steps to install soft close drawer slides are to remove the old slides, attach the cabinet and drawer members, test, and adjust if necessary. It’s crucial to have the right tools and hardware for a smooth installation process.

Installation Tips and Best Practices

When installing soft close drawer slides, we recommend reading the instructions carefully before beginning. Ensure that the drawer and cabinet members are correctly spaced and leveled. When attaching the drawer members, make sure they are parallel to the cabinet member to ensure the drawer operates smoothly.

You can also use spacers to help position the drawer slide accurately and prevent binding.

### Types of Soft Close Drawer Slides

There are two types of soft close drawer slides: side mount and undermount. Side mount drawer slides are the most common and easiest to install. Undermount slides are hidden underneath the drawer and provide a sleeker look but are more challenging to install.

Undermount slides also require a bit more precision in the installation process. It’s critical to ensure that the slides are perfectly aligned and leveled to avoid any issues with the drawer’s movement.

Choosing the Right Soft Close Drawer Slides

When selecting soft close drawer slides, consider the size and weight of your drawers. Full extension slides are suitable for heavy items as they offer maximum support. Partial extension makes sense for lighter items and is ideal for use in the kitchen or bathroom.

Another important factor to consider is the type of material used in constructing the slide. Ball-bearing slides and epoxy-coated slides are more durable and smoother than their counterparts.

Choose a reputable brand with excellent reviews for the best results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can you use side mount drawer slides as undermount?

A. No, it is not recommended to use side mount drawer slides as undermount because the measurements and installation methods are entirely different.

Q. Is it necessary to remove the drawer before installing the slides?

A. Yes, it is necessary to remove the drawer before installing the slides to ensure proper measurements and positioning.

Q. What is the weight capacity of soft close drawer slides?

A. The weight capacity of soft close drawer slides depends on the quality and type. However, most standard drawer slides can support up to 100 lbs.

Q. Can I install soft close drawer slides on an existing cabinet?

A. Yes, you can install soft close drawer slides on an existing cabinet. Just make sure to select the right slides for your cabinet and follow the installation instructions carefully.

Conclusion of How to Install Soft Close Drawer Slides

Now that you have a better idea of how to install soft close drawer slides, you can easily upgrade your drawers with this modern feature. Remember to follow the installation instructions carefully, choose the right type of slide for your needs, and have the necessary tools on hand. With a bit of patience and persistence, you’ll have smooth and quiet drawers in no time!
