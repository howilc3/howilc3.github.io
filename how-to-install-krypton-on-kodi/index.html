How to Troubleshoot Krypton Installation Errors

Some users may experience errors during the installation process. Here are some tips on troubleshooting your installation:

Check Your Device’s Compatibility

Before downloading Kodi Krypton, make sure that your device’s operating system is compatible with the software.

Clear Your Device’s Cache

Your device’s cache might be clogged, causing Kodi Krypton’s installation error. Clear your device’s cache and try reinstalling the software.

Disable Your Firewall and Antivirus Software

Some antivirus software may block the installation process. Disable your firewall and antivirus software temporarily, then try reinstalling Kodi Krypton. Don’t forget to turn them back on after successfully installing Kodi.
