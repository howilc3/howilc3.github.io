If you’ve recently purchased an LG smart TV, you’re probably excited about the endless entertainment possibilities it offers. However, installing apps on your LG smart TV may seem daunting at first, especially if you’re not familiar with the process. But don’t worry! In this article, we’ll provide step-by-step instructions on how to install apps on LG smart TV and answer any questions you may have along the way.

Connecting your LG smart TV to the internet is essential if you want to take full advantage of its features, including installing apps. But once you’ve connected your TV to your home network, the process is relatively straightforward. However, navigating through the app store can be confusing, and selecting the wrong app can waste valuable time. We’ve got you covered! Our guide will help you avoid common pitfalls and get you to your favorite content in no time.

To install apps on your LG smart TV, follow these five easy steps:

Step 1: Access the LG content store

Using your remote, select the LG content store icon from the home screen. The LG content store contains a vast selection of apps, games, and other digital content. It’s important to note that some apps require a subscription or purchase to access the content.

Step 2: Browse the available apps

From the content store, browse through the available apps. You can search by category or type the name of the app you’re looking for in the search bar. Once you’ve found an app you want to install, select it, and click on the “install” button.

Step 3: Sign in to your account (if necessary)

Some apps may require a subscription or a linked account to access the content. If this is the case, follow the on-screen prompts to sign in to your account. The process will vary depending on the app and the device you’re using.

Step 4: Wait for the installation to finish

After clicking the “install” button, wait for the app to download and install. Depending on the app’s size, this process may take a few minutes. Once the installation is complete, the app will appear on the home screen.

Step 5: Access your newly installed app

From the home screen, scroll through until you find the newly installed app. Press “OK” on your remote to open and enjoy your new content.

How to move and delete apps on LG smart TV

If you need to rearrange your app layout or remove an app from your LG smart TV, follow these simple steps:

To move an app, press and hold the “OK” button on your remote until the app icon begins to jiggle. Then, use the directional arrows to move it to your desired location.

To delete an app, press and hold the “OK” button on your remote until the option to delete the app appears. Select “delete,” and the app will be removed from your TV.

FAQs about how to install apps on LG smart TV

Q: Can I install apps on LG smart TV without an internet connection?

A: No. Installing apps on LG smart TV requires an internet connection.

Q: Can I install apps on LG smart TV from sources other than the LG content store?

A: Yes, but it’s not recommended. Installing apps from third-party sources can be risky and may not function correctly on your LG smart TV.

Q: Is there a limit to how many apps I can install on my LG smart TV?

A: No, there’s no limit to the number of apps you can install on your LG smart TV. However, keep in mind that the TV’s storage capacity may limit the number of apps you can download.

Q: Is it possible to update apps on LG smart TV?

A: Yes. LG smart TVs automatically download app updates, but you can also manually update them using the same process used to install apps.

Conclusion of how to install apps on LG smart TV

Installing apps on your LG smart TV is quick and easy using these five steps. Shopping in the LG content store provides the latest and safest selection of digital options with endless possibilities. Once you have access to your favorite apps, you can enjoy entertainment your way on a beautiful and intuitive screen.
